About the game

Welcome to the multiplayer, open world of MiniState. Compete with other players to build the greatest nation the world will ever know. Wage war, forge alliances, conquer lands, and advance your technology to make your mark in MiniState history.

  • EXPLORE: Explore a massive, open world and marvel at the wonders and ruins of competing nations.
  • CONQUER: Organize armies to control strategic resources and trade routes.
  • ALLIANCES: Forge alliances with friends to surround and defeat foes together.
  • TRADE: Use your unique geography to trade with other nations for vital resources.
  • TECHNOLOGY: Research modern weaponry and advance through history.
  • CITIES: Organize your structures to optimize industrial output and prosperity. Make your cities the envy of your neighbors.
  • EMPIRE: Enslave other players and make them bow to your power.
  • DESTROY: Grind your foes to dust, make them disappear from history.
